Welcome to the Philippines Association of Practitioners of Student Affairs and Services, Inc.

The Philippine Association of Practitioners of Student Affairs and Services, Inc. is the professional organization of student affairs and services administrator and practitioners in the Philippines, dedicated to the pursuit of excellence in student affairs and services work. It is an effective channel through which student affairs practitioners can develop and enhance their capabilities.

PAPSAS, Inc. is committed to the formation of the Filipino educators through the conduct of effective and relevant programs and services addressing student issues and student affairs development. PAPSAS, Inc. is dedicated in building the competencies of its members through programs and services it offers.

PAPSAS Research Conference 2024

From July 17-19, 2024, at Iloilo City, we gathered some of the brightest minds in SAS Unit for an enriching exchange of ideas and insights. The conference featured insightful keynote speeches, engaging panel discussions, and innovative research presentations that showcased the cutting-edge work being done in our community.

A huge thank you to all our participants, speakers, sponsors, and volunteers who made this event possible. Your dedication and enthusiasm truly made this conference a resounding success!

𝐇𝐈𝐆𝐇𝐋𝐈𝐆𝐇𝐓𝐒 𝐎𝐅 𝐒𝐔𝐂𝐂𝐄𝐒𝐒 | 𝟐𝟎𝟐𝟒 𝐌𝐢𝐝𝐲𝐞𝐚𝐫 𝐈𝐧𝐭𝐞𝐫𝐚𝐜𝐭𝐢𝐯𝐞 𝐘𝐨𝐮𝐭𝐡 𝐅𝐨𝐫𝐮𝐦

What an inspiring three days it’s been! The 2024 Midyear Interactive Youth Forum, held from October 16-18 at the scenic Bohol Plaza Resort, brought together hundreds of passionate student leaders, volunteers and SAS Practitioners.

Through engaging discussions, workshops, and interactive activities, participants were empowered to make a difference in their communities, advocating for the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) focused on poverty, health, quality education, gender equality, climate action, and peace.

We are incredibly proud of our delegates who showcased commitment, leadership, and empathy, all while forging meaningful connections and collaborations. This event has truly ignited a new wave of changemakers ready to take action.

We are thrilled to announce the phenomenal success of the second run of the 𝟭𝟱𝘁𝗵 𝗜𝗻𝘁𝗲𝗿𝗮𝗰𝘁𝗶𝘃𝗲 𝗬𝗼𝘂𝘁𝗵 𝗙𝗼𝗿𝘂𝗺 organized by PAPSAS, Inc. held at 𝘾𝙧𝙤𝙬𝙣 𝙇𝙚𝙜𝙖𝙘𝙮 𝙃𝙤𝙩𝙚𝙡, 𝘽𝙖𝙜𝙪𝙞𝙤 𝘾𝙞𝙩𝙮, from 𝗔𝗽𝗿𝗶𝗹 𝟭𝟳-𝟭𝟵, 𝟮𝟬𝟮𝟰. Following the outstanding response and impact of the first run in March, which set the bar high, this second run exceeded all expectations and has become the most attended and successful edition of the IYF to date!

We were honored to welcome 480 passionate and dedicated student leaders and SAS Practitioners from 79 universities across the region. Their enthusiasm, energy, and commitment to the theme "Systems and Anticipatory Thinking for Sustainable Student Activities and Stewardship" were truly inspiring and made this event an incredible success.

Throughout the forum, participants engaged in thought-provoking discussions, interactive workshops, and collaborative activities aimed at fostering sustainable student activities and promoting effective stewardship. The exchange of ideas, experiences, and best practices was enriching and empowering, leaving a lasting impact on everyone involved.

We extend our heartfelt gratitude to all the participants, speakers, sponsors, and volunteers who contributed to making this event a resounding success. Your dedication and passion for creating positive change in student communities are truly commendable.

Let's continue to harness the power of collaboration, innovation, and anticipatory thinking to build a brighter and more sustainable future for all. Together, we can make a difference!
